I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Social Robotics Lab within the Department of Computer Science at Yale University.
My advisor is Brian Scassellati.

I'm interested in machine learning techniques applied to robotics, reinforcement learning for continuous state and action spaces, and policy search methods. Before coming to Yale, I completed a Master of Engineering from the University of Bristol. Take a look at my CV for more details!
